Framework & IP
STAVR.
Sequential Task Attention, Verification & Recovery
A framework for encoding how experts execute tasks reliably under uncertainty by formalising attention, verification, and recovery. Independent of domain or platform.

Most AI and robotics systems focus on two layers: perception (what is happening) and control (how to act). Between them sits a third, largely unaddressed problem: expert execution logic under uncertainty.

STAVR is that missing layer. It is a universal skill execution layer that other systems can consume. Not a robotics control system, not a workflow engine, not a model training pipeline.
